This page has a calculator useful for answering those questions that come up when you're in talks or writing proposals, like *'How many kpc correspond to 1 arcminute at redshift 3?'*, *'How bright is an L\* galaxy at redshift 1.5?'* or *'What is the lookback time to redshift 2?'*. For a nice introduction to calculating useful cosmological quantities see [Hogg (1999)](http://arxiv.org/abs/astro-ph/9905116) and the references therein. The default parameters below are taken from the [WMAP7 results](http://lambda.gsfc.nasa.gov/product/map/current/parameters.cfm) ([ADS](http://adsabs.harvard.edu/cgi-bin/bib_query?arXiv:1001.4744)). A flat cosmology is assumed.
